About Coach Sam

Coaching With A Plan

Sam has spent nearly a decade around the game — a five‑year collegiate defenseman turned full‑time coach. Today he's Head Coach of Homestead Lacrosse and Director of Revolt Lacrosse, where his teams are built on fundamentals, competitiveness, and players who steadily get better.

Every private session is tailored to one athlete and one set of goals. We start with an honest look at where you are, build a simple plan, and put in focused reps — whether you're brand new to the sport or chasing a varsity spot.
